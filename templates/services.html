Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about our logistics and freight services.

What areas do you cover?

We provide logistics solutions across Kenya, East Africa, and international trade hubs. Whether local or global, we ensure your cargo reaches its destination safely and on time.

Can I track my shipment in real time?

Yes. Our online tracking system allows you to monitor your shipment 24/7, from pickup to final delivery, giving you full visibility and peace of mind.

Do you handle customs clearance?

Absolutely. Our experienced team manages all customs documentation and compliance requirements, ensuring smooth clearance and avoiding unnecessary delays.

What types of goods can you transport?

We handle a wide range of cargo including consumer goods, industrial equipment, agricultural products, and fragile items. Specialized handling and packaging are available upon request.

How do I request a quote?

You can request a quote directly through our website by filling out the Request a Quote form. Provide your shipment details and our team will get back to you with a tailored solution.
